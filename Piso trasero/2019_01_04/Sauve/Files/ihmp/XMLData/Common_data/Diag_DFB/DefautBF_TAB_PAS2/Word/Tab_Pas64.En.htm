Explanation of the defect

Cause(s)/ Solution(s)

CONFIGURATION DEFECT: DIAG.CodeDef = 64

 

- There is a configuration conflict of the function box:

* Number of directions and/ or of roller speeds

* Number of overshoot detectors

 

 

 

 - Configuration of overshoot detectors: DEPAS (OVERSHOOT)

* DEPAS = 1  =>  No overshoot detector

* DEPAS = 2  =>  1 Front overshoot detector

* DEPAS = 3  =>  1 Rear overshoot detector

* DEPAS = 4  =>  1 FR overshoot detector and 1 RR overshoot detector

 

- Configuration of the direction of rotation of the rollers: SENS (DIRECTION)

       * SENS = 1 => 1 Direction of the Rollers

    * SENS = 2 => 2 Direction of Rotation of the Rollers. Unloading on Table n-1

  * SENS = 3 => 2 Direction of Rotation of the Rollers. Unloading on Table n+1

 

- Configuration of the speed of rollers: VITE (SPEED)

   * SENS =  1   => 1 Speed of FR Rollers

   * SENS =  2   => 2 Speed of FR Rollers

      * SENS =  5   => 1 Speed of FR and 1 Speed of RR of rollers

      * SENS =  6   => 2 Speeds of FR and 1 Speed of RR of rollers

      * SENS =  9   => 1 Speed of FR and 2 Speeds of RR of rollers

      * SENS =  10   => 2 Speeds of FR and 2 Speeds of RR of rollers

Procedure for intervention and recycling

Ensure safety AND Shift to manual mode.

-       Change the API program as a result.

-       Press the BADI defect(s) cancellation push-button.

-       Turn the selector to AUTO, press the start cycle push-button.
