Explanation of the defect

Cause(s)/ Solution(s)

DEFECT OF REAR CONTROL SENSOR(S): DIAG.CodeDef = 8

 

-       The last transit has not permit to control the good functioning of at least one sensor at the rear of the handling element

See the IHMINFO value to find the solution:

 

4 – Check the operation of the DPR sensor, adjust the sensor or replace it if required.

 

5 – Check the operation of the DPVR sensor, adjsut the sensor or replace it if required.

 

6 – Check the operation of the DDPR sensor, adjust the sensor or replace it if required.

 

8 – Check the support and/or the adjustment of all the sensors because the sensors were detected individually. Check the connection of the sensors.

Procedure for intervention and recycling

Ensure safety AND Shift to manual mode.

Solve the cause(s) of the defect.

-       In the operation/ Maintenance page, set the CDR  memory of the defective handling element.

-       If the cause(s) of the defect are solved, IHMINFO is set to 0.

-       Press the BADI defect(s) cancellation push-button.

-       Turn the selector to AUTO, press the Start cycle push-button.
