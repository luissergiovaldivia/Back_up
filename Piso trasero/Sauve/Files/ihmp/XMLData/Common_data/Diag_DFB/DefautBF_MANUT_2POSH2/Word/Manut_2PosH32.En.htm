On-board 24V Defect :  

 

 * #Diag.CodeDef = 32

 

   * #Diag.Ihmdf24VE at 1

 

- The PLC controller detected the loss of the 24v on board

 

- Check the 24V power supply circuit breaker of the cross transfer or the turn table.

 

- Check whether 24v is present in the fixed switchbox

 

- Check whether the wires are cut or earthed in the cable for the turn table or in the cable for a cross transfer
