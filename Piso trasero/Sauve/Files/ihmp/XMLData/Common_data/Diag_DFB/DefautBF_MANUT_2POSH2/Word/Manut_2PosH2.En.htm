Forward Position Control Defect:

 

* #Diag.CodeDef = 2

 

* #Diag.Ihmdfposa at 1

 

- The Forward Movement can be controlled but it is not controlled at the end of the reference time

 

 

 

1- Check:

- The operation of low speed detectors and End of Forward movement

- The condition of Lead strips

- Check the condition of the Inverter

- Check the Power Supply and the motor

 

2- In Manual Operation, Bring the cross transfer or the turn table to a known position:

- Cancel the Defect

 

3- Validate the Auto Operation
