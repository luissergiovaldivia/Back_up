Explanation of the defect

Cause(s)/ Solution(s)

RESET DEFECT OF REAR SENSOR(S) : DIAG.CodeDef = 512

 

- During the last transit, one of the sensors at the rear of the handling element is not reset to 0.

 

See the IHMINFO value to find the solution:

 

4 – Check the operation of the DPR sensor, clean the sensor or replace it if required.

 

5 – Check the operation of the DPVR sensor, clean the sensor or replace it if required.

 

6 – Check the operation of the DDPR sensor, clean the sensor or replace it if required.

Procedure for intervention and recycling

Ensure safety AND Shift to manual mode.

Solve the cause(s) of the defect.

-       If the cause(s) of the defect are solved, IHMINFO is set to 0.

-       Press the BADI defect(s) cancellation push-button.

-       Turn the selector to AUTO, press the Start cycle push-button.
