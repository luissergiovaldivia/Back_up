Explanation of the defect

Cause(s)/ Solution(s)

FRONT OVERSHOOT DEFECT:

DIAG.CodeDef = 16

 

- The support stopped for the forward overshoot detector.

See theIHMINFO value to find the solution:

 

1 – The DPA sensor was not detected. Check the sensor. Check the support.

 

2 – There is no change in speed before stoppage because the DPVA sensor was not detected. Check the sensor. Check the support.

 

10 – The stoppage sensor is too close to the DDPA sensor.

 Or:

-       Check the status of the support.

-       Check the status of the equipment.

-       Check the inverter setting.

Intervention and recycling procedure

Ensure safety AND Shift to manual mode.

Solve the cause(s) of the defect.

-       Have the support move back to disengage the DDPA sensor.

-       If the cause(s) of the defect are solved, IHMINFO is set to 0.

-       Press the BADI defect(s) cancellation push-button.

-       Turn the selector to AUTO, press the start cycle push-button.
